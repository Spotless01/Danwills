About Us

Discover the story behind Danwilhs Fragrance Hub.

Danwilhs Fragrance Hub is a fast-rising and highly influential fragrance brand in Ghana, founded and led by Daniel Asante. The brand has positioned itself as a trusted destination for authentic, high-quality perfumes, serving both retail and wholesale customers across the country.

At its core, Danwilhs Fragrance Hub is not just a perfume business—it is a movement shaping fragrance culture in Ghana, especially in the growing market of perfume oils and niche scents.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become one of Africa’s leading fragrance distributors, known for authenticity, uniqueness, and market influence.

Mission: To provide customers with original, long-lasting, and exclusive fragrances while educating the market on quality, authenticity, and the true value of perfumes.

Business Model

Danwilhs Fragrance Hub operates on a dual-structured model:

  • Retail Sales: Supplying individual customers with premium perfumes and oils
  • Wholesale Distribution: Partnering with resellers and vendors across Ghana

Product Range

  • Designer-inspired perfumes
  • Niche and rare fragrances
  • Undiluted perfume oils
  • Unisex, masculine, and feminine scents

We focus on unique and uncommon fragrances to help customers stand out.

Market Position & Influence

  • Pioneers of undiluted perfume oil trends in Ghana
  • Trusted for authentic products only
  • Educating customers on fake vs original perfumes
  • Strong reputation in the fragrance community

Customer Experience

  • Personalized fragrance recommendations
  • Long-lasting scents suited for Ghana’s climate
  • Reliable delivery
  • Strong customer relationships

Competitive Advantage

  • Early access to trending fragrances
  • Strong street credibility
  • Deep perfume knowledge
  • Health-conscious and safe products
  • Loyal customer base

Our Philosophy

“Perfume is not just about smelling good — it’s about presence, identity, and making a lasting impression.”

Future Goals

  • Expansion across West Africa
  • Partnerships with international perfume houses
  • Building a fragrance empire brand
  • Launching private-label scents
